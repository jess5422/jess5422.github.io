Dismembered Robot

This fined-grained drawing has been made by Phlegm. As explained on this website , the artist's name would refer to one of the four humours in ancient Greek medecine. Phlegm was related to an apathetic and unemotional temperament. In fact, most of Phlegm's pieces of streetart are black and white, but are unlikely to leave you without emotion!

Plegm is auto-publishing comics, and has created a book "Everything There Ever Was and Will Be". This title echoes one of his painting , sold for the occasion.

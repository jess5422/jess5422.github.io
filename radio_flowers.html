Radioactive Flowers

This painting, "Say it with flowers" is from Otto Schade. Global warming, pollution and concern for the natural environment are expressed in a poetice manner. The dark shape of the tiny girl is made salient by the vivid orange background. Could it represents the sun, some fire, both?

Otto Schade have created several scenes using this orange and black circle design. Other pieces of his work are characterized by the presence of ribbons. You can find out more on his website and on his Facebook page.

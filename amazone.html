Amazone

On a wooden door in Brick Lane, one can find this great portrait of a wild woman. It has been painted by Dan Kitchener. We found it in July, 2015 and one year and a half later, it is still there, even though a bit overpainted by some graffitis. The artist is selling a piece of art, "Tokyo Blue", that looks quite the same.

Under this colorfull portrait, Dan has added around October, 2015, a street scene. You can have an overview of the full door and see Dan at work here .

Observing Heart

This graffiti has been found in Brick Lane (Hanbury Street), on a market day :) This is an awesome piece of artwork from Alex Diaz and Elian Chali. The heart itself is composed of many details and fine-grained sketches. You almost feel that it is beating on the wall and scrutinizing you while you are passing around. This piece is recovered by another graff.

Alex Diaz is a Puerto Rican artist, who started to create murals in 2010. You can find more about his work on his own Facebook page , as well as on this article .
